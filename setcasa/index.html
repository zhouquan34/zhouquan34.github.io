SETCASA Poster Competition 2023

We are excited to announce that the annual poster competition of SETCASA (Southeast Texas Chapter of American Statistical Association) will be held on May 3 at Texas A&M University. This is a great opportunity for junior statisticians to showcase your research and network with your peers. To participate, use the link below to register and submit your abstract by Apr 1. Selected applicants will be invited to present their posters at the competition.
There is no registration fee, and we expect that free lunch will be provided to all attendees. We look forward to seeing you there!

Eligibility: Everyone is welcome to attend the event, but to participate in the competition, you need to be an undergraduate, graduate student, or you obtained the most recent PhD degree after Jan 2019.

Awards: We expect that cash prizes will be awarded to the top 3 posters.

Time: 11am to 2pm, May 3 Wednesday, 2023.

Location: Room 453 (Fisher Bowl), John R. Blocker Building.

Address: 155 Ireland Street, College Station, TX 77843.
